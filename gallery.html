About Hindustan Marble & Granites

We are one of Chandigarh's reliable suppliers of marble, granite and tiles. Located in the Dhanas area, our store has been serving homeowners, builders, architects and contractors who want quality stone products at honest prices.

Whether you are building a new home, renovating your kitchen or setting up a commercial space, we have the right stone for you. Our collection includes Italian marble, Indian granite, vitrified floor tiles, designer wall tiles and much more.

We believe that a beautiful space starts with the right material. That's why we personally handpick our stock to make sure every piece meets our quality standards.
